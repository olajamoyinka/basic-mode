Concentration

A good driver must concentrate while on steering, he should not be looking here and there. He must not salute somebody while the vehicle is on motion

Observation

A good looking driver must be able to observe something or to observe the bad roads.He should be able to know where something is going to happen. He must observe to know if his vehicle is going to spoil before final damage. If your steering is bad you have the feeling. If you have human feeling, then you are a good driver
